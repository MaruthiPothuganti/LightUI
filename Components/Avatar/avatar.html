Avatar

Avatars are images that users can set as their profile picture. They're always going to be rounded or squares. They can be custom photos, uploaded by users, or generated as Identicons as a placeholder. They can also be initials of the username. Different type of avatars are as follows.

Rounded Avatar

Rounded Avatar's are the avatars which are circular and are available in different sizes like extra small, small, medium and large by adding avatar being the general class name for all avatar-rd and adding av-xs,av-s,av-m,av-l as additional Class names respectively.

Squared Avatar

Squared Avatar's are the avatars which are Squared and are available in different sizes just like rounded avatars. They have a generic class name as avatar-sq. They are also resized by adding the same additional classnames as of rounded avatar's.
